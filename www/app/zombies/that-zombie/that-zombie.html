Exo zombies

The zombies were created by a Manticore strike. In the intel from Bio Lab, Irons states that Manticore has a second effect to regenerate cells that are possibly dead, but this was unconfirmed.

Zombies with-out exo suits, they move at different speeds and will attack the closest player. Watch out for Zombies with exo suits as they can do more things than normal ones. Sense they have an exo suit they do more damage than normal ones and can boost jump to a player to hurt them. Just like when you have an exo suit, you do more damage. They also move at different speeds.

Hosts

Hosts can be easily distinguished from other zombies by a much different appearance; their appearance is a "lumpy" zombie that emits green Manticore clouds from the mouth. They appear to have a needle injected into their infected right hand signifying the zombie was tested on by Atlas scientists.

Hosts appear during special rounds along with other zombies. When hitting a player, that player becomes "infected", and must use the Decontamination Zone within 60 seconds or they will die. The player(s) screen will start to darken when the infected timer has reached 5 seconds and will slowly turn into a zombie. While infected, any hit from a Host will deduct five seconds from the timer. Once the player is cured by the Zone, they can be re-infected as many times as possible.

Once the last zombie of the Hosts round is killed, it will drop a Full Reload power-up, and any infected player will be cured without needing to go to the Decontamination Zone. When the player kills a Host, it will explode and leak out green fluid.

When it is a Host round, Angie will give the player(s) a warning over the intercom. "Attention, Security has been breached" signifying the Hosts are coming.

Scurity Dogs

As suggested by their name, as well as being mentioned by Kahn, they are dogs that were used by the security and were zombified.

Security Dogs appear during special rounds (the announcer will say it in the beginning of the round). The dogs will come out of their kennels and attack the players. When the last one is killed, it will drop a Full Reload power-up.

Dogs can spawn either as regular ones, or as special ones that behave like EMZs and whose attacks will disable the player's exo. There is also a type of dog that behaves like Exploders.

While rather weak opponents, they deal the same damage per attack as regular zombies and are faster, which can make them dangerous, especially the EMZ-like ones.

Chargers

Chargers appear randomly during rounds. They can be easily noticed by the red mist coming out their eyes, mouth and body.

Chargers are faster than normal zombies. They run faster and tend more regularly to use their exosuit to jump towards the player and attack them. Chargers can be dangerous as they can easily reach a player and hit them.

EMZs

EMZs have exo suits, and can be easily distinguished from other zombies by electricity on their body. They seem to spawn after round 4.

When a player is hit by the EMZ or if a player attempts to boost away from one, it will produce an EMP, shutting down the player's exo. The exo suit is disabled for five seconds, and during these five seconds, the player will not be able to use any exo suit ability. Their view will also be blurred. When an EMZ dies the dead corpse will leak out a blue fluid of some sort, possibly the electricity it has been exposed to. It is best to kill an EMZ once spotted, as disabling exo suits can be problematic in higher rounds, as it prevents the player from using it to quickly escape.

Exploders

Exploders can be distinguished from regular zombies by green liquid around their body and on fire. They spawn during regular rounds, and can spawn as soon as round 1.

When approached, the Exploder will, as its name indicates, explode. The explosion deals small damage to nearby players but will one-hit kill nearby zombies. If the player does not kill an Exploder for a period of time, it will charge up and when killed the blast radius will increase and possibly hurt or in rare cases down any player in the vicinity

Spikers

Spikers are similar to Chargers, but they have spikes surrounding its body.
The spikes act like armor so they are tougher to kill.

Don't get near them because the spikes do hurt you when the zombie touches you.

When killed, they will spread spikes around it. Which the Spikes will damage players as well as zombies close to the Spiker.
So try killing them far away.

Acid Drippers

Acid Drippers, or Ozzers, can be noticed by acid that flows from their body. Oozers will spawn along with regular zombies as soon as round 5.

Oozers constantly drop puddles of acid to the ground. Walking over these will damage the player.

So its recommended to kill them as soon as the spawn because the longer they live the more puddles of acid there will be.

Goliath Exo Zombie

The Goliath Zombie is heavily mutated and bloated into a large set of muscle in a goliath suit. It carries a huge riot shield and a makeshift hammer as its weapon made out of a drain cover with a concrete arm attached into it.

The Goliath appears every 10 rounds, and will spawn with other types Zombies. Once killed, it will drop a Full Reload.

The Goliath is extremely dangerous when faced alone or with another companion, since only two hits will be enough to kill a player. Fighting the zombie with multiple teammates is the best option to quickly take it down. It can activate an EMP when approached, which will shut the player's exo down. It can fire heat-seeking missiles that will cause an EMP when detonated as well.

Blinkers

Teleporting Zombies teleport to the player in short distances. They are wearing blue exo suit which makes them teleport.

Its best to shoot them fast so you dont get trapped when they teleport to you.

The Teleporting Zombies are used for the easter egg to collect the teleport grenade like things.

Atlas Strike Team

The Atlas Strike Team spawns when there is a gas bomb. They teleport in and try to defend the bomb from the players.

All the Atlas Soldiers have Camouflage, so zombies can't attack them. When they die, they sometimes drop a Camouflage that will last for 10 seconds.

Some Atlas Soldiers have Shields and a ohm. The rest just have an ohm. After round 20 a Goliath will start spawning everytime a gas bomb spawns.

Fused Zombies

A Fused Zombie is two zombies in one. Blinker fused with a Spiker makes a purple looking zombie. Meatbags fused with EMZs, looks like a Meatbag but has electricity like a EMZ and others.

Meatbags

Meatbags are zombies that were Atlas Officers with tactical armor. They have more health because of the armor they have. Also they have high resistance against headshot, due to the helmet they are wearing. The helmet does come off after its been shot a few times.
