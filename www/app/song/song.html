The song will activated when you press "X" on 3 medical tools (Wrench, Saw, and a Bone Saw).


-The first one is the Wrench. It is in the Decontamination room, on the right of the upgrade station. You will see it on the wall.

-The second one is the Bone Saw. It is the Holding room. On the left of the upgrade station. You will see it at the end of the person's feet.

-The third one is the Saw. It is located in the Exo Testing Room. You will see it on the left of the experimental Upgrade Station. It will be at the open door in between the crack.
The song will activated when you press "X" on 3 Golden Rockets.


-The first one is in a shelf facing the Upgrade Station in the Value Voltage.

-The second one is on the ground, to the right of the Exo Slam machine.

-The third one is on the rooftop of the Burger Town, behind a fence (it can be best seen by crouching).
The song will activated when you press "X" on 3 small sharks.


-The first one is in the Hanger. You can find it on top of Captain DJ's locker. The lockers are right next to the 3D Printer.

-The second one is between the Hanger and the Gun Deck. The Shark is loacted in the room that goes to the Hanger and the Gun Deck, on a shelf all the way to the bottom.

-The third one is in the Lift. You can find the shark hiding in a corner. Climb on the shark tanks and go to the one closest to Exo Reload. The shark will be located by the rail.
The song will activated when you press "X" on 3 Sting Rays.


-The first one is in the Concierge tunnel drop. The sting ray will be on one of the sides of the wall.

-The second one is loacated in the Galleria. Coming from the Great Hall into the Galleria, there will be a broken fish tank. The sting ray will be sitting on a rock.

-The third one is on the top floor in the Tidal Generator. Jump on top off the teleporter and it will be on the side closest to the ocean.
