Oz

Rank:Janitor
Sex:Male
Hieght:6'0"
Hair:Grey(balding)
Eyes:Brown
Actor:John Malkovich
Little is known about Oz's life before working at the Atlas Corporation. What is known, is that Oz was formerly a soldier who participated in a war. While it is unknown whose side Oz fought for, the experience was mostly unpleasant for him. According to Captain Lennox, Oz was also previously a member of the Sentinel Task Force, up to the point when they witnessed the zombie infection begin in New Baghdad. He then supposedly left Sentinel, and went to work at an Atlas research facility, as a janitor, to avoid the complexity of his previous life. On one night, while performing his usual clean up duty, Oz spotted streaks of blood on the ground, along with several broken glass casings and some dead bodies. Afterwards, a zombie outbreak occured in the facility, forcing him to evacuate. He arrived at the helicopter landing pad along with three other Atlas employees, Lilith, Decker, and Kahn. The rescue Warbird was attacked by zombies and crashes down, forcing Oz and the others to team up to survive against the onslaught of zombies. In an attempt to escape, all four survivors retrieved a communication box from the Warbird, as well as their employee keycards, and used them to call in a second Warbird for extraction. However, as the Warbird approached the facility, it was shot down by an Atlas care package, leaving the four survivors stranded. They eventually made a last stand against the zombies, but were defeated. Atlas forces arrived and saved them in the nick of time, then transported them to a facility located near a Burger Town restaurant. Some times later, they recovered and found themselves in the midst of another zombie infection. Not only did they have to fend off the undead, but they also had to rescue civilians trapped in the vicinity of Burger Town. The four survivors eventually found a way to activate the Burger Town mascot, Bubby, which either came to life and aided them in battle, or just simply exploded. While doing this, they also unknowingly launched a missile. After doing this, they were stopped on the roof of the Burger Town restaurant and were taken captive by Sentinel forces. Their leader, Lennox, pointed a pistol at them and claimed one of the four must die. Oz is then executed in front of the other three, with Lennox justifying his actions due to Oz being the source of the infection. He then ordered his men to load Oz onto a chopper, while Decker, Kahn, and Lilith were then brought to an Atlas aircraft carrier out in the ocean. While being carried to the ship, Oz's body began to reanimate. While on the ship, Lennox and the Atlas employees discovered several diagrams depicting Oz as "Patient Zero", hinting at a possibility of Oz having been experimented on by Atlas Corporation before. They also found several data tablets, which contained the journal of the ship's former captain. When they inserted these tablets into the vault at the Gun Deck, the A.I. of the ship would then proceed to read them out in Oz's voice. After collecting all the tablets and discovering the story, the crew then decided to sink the ship to the bottom of the ocean. However, before they could be extracted in an emergency pod, they encountered Oz, having completely reanimated as a zombie.

An-G

type: A.I
An-G is an A.I and she announces to the players when a power-up is picked up, when a round of Hosts or Security Dogs spawn, when a 3D Printer breaks, when a door is opened, and when a Power Switch is activated. She also converses with the characters when they talk to her.

Survivors

Sex:Male
The player has the opportunity to escort them to Eagle One's Warbird, where the survivor(s) will be extracted. This will grant the players an upgrade for their weapons. If a Survivor is highlighted in red, they are about to die. If the player fails to save one, all power on the map will be shut down for 60 seconds, and the power switches will have to be manually turned on again afterwards.

When you turn the power switches back on, you don't get credits.

Eagle One

Sex:Male
Eagle One is the call sign of a Warbird. He tasks the players with escorting Survivors to his Warbird. When the survivors reach the extraction point, Eagle One will arrive, gunning down zombies in the area, and will extract the survivors using a rope. He regularly contacts the players, first at the beginning of the game where he calls another helicopter to extract the team, and during the game where he will randomly detect and locate survivors in the area.

Lennox

Sex:Male Hair:Dark Brown Eyes:Dark Brown Actor:Bruce Campbell
Not much is known about Lennox before the zombie infection began. What is known is that he has been keeping track of four Atlas employees - Oz, Lilith Swann, Jim Decker and Kahn - for an unknown amount of time, possibly before their attempt to survive the outbreak at the Atlas research facility. When the four of them were brought back to an Atlas facility near a Burger Town restaurant, Lennox continued to monitor them, up to the point where they recovered and fought the undead forces in Burger Town. Lennox, going by the callsign Eagle One, sent out a message to the four survivors, asking them to hold out while he brings in a bigger chopper to evacuate them. He later requested them to help extract several civilians, who were trapped within the area. Each time, Lennox would arrive in a Warbird, extracting the civilian while providing assistance to the group. Eventually, he arrived in a Razorback VTOL, along with his Sentinel troops, and saved them from the last of the zombie horde, but held them at gunpoint. Upon meeting the survivors, he claimed that one of them "has to go". True to his words, Lennox then executed Oz in front of the other three, claiming that he was the source of the infection. He then had the other three injected with anesthetic, then transported them to an Atlas carrier ship. Once on board, he led Lilith, Kahn and Decker inside, where they found several diagrams depicting Oz as "Patient Zero", hinting at a possibility of Atlas Corporation having done experiments on him. After retrieving several data tablets containing the journals of the ship's captain, they then proceeded to sink the ship. However, before they could extract in an emergency pod, they encountered Oz, who had reanimated as a zombie.
