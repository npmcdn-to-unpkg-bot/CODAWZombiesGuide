Oz

Rank:Janitor
Sex:Male
Hieght:6'0"
Hair:Grey(balding)
Eyes:Brown
Actor:John Malkovich
Oz works as a janitor in an Atlas research facility. On one night, while performing his usual clean up duty, Oz spots streaks of blood on the ground, along with several broken glass casings and some dead bodies. Afterwards, a zombie outbreak occurs in the facility, forcing him to evacuate. He arrives at the helicopter landing pad along with three other Atlas employees, Lilith, Decker, and Kahn. The rescue Warbird is attacked by zombies and crashes down, forcing Oz and the others to team up to survive against the onslaught of zombies. In an attempt to escape, all four survivors retrieve their employee keycards, and use them to call in a second Warbird for extraction. However, as the Warbird approaches the facility, it is shot down by an unknown rocket, leaving the four survivors stranded. Oz' fate, along with the others, is currently unknown

In an attempt to escape, all four survivors retrieve their employee keycards, and use them to call in a second Warbird for extraction. However, as the Warbird approaches the facility, it is shot down by an Orbital Care Package, leaving the four survivors stranded at the overrun facility. Lilith's fate, along with the others, is currently unknown.

Angie

type: A.I
She is the Exo Zombies equivalent of the Demonic Announcer. She announces to the players when a power-up is picked up, when a round of Hosts or Security Dogs spawn, when a 3D Printer breaks, when a door is opened, and when a Power Switch is activated. She also converses with the characters when they talk to her.

Survivors

Sex:Male
The player has the opportunity to escort them to Eagle One's Warbird, where the survivor(s) will be extracted. This will grant the players an upgrade for their weapons. If a Survivor is highlighted in red, they are about to die. If the player fails to save one, all power on the map will be shut down for 60 seconds, and the power switches will have to be manually turned on again afterwards.

When you turn the power switches back on, you dont get credits.

Eagle One

Sex:Male
Eagle One is the callsign of a Warbird. He tasks the players with escorting Survivors to his Warbird. When the survivors reach the extraction point, Eagle One will arrive, gunning down zombies in the area, and will extract the survivors using a rope. He regularly contacts the players, first at the beginning of the game where he calls another helicopter to extract the team, and during the game where he will randomly detect and locate survivors in the area.

Sentinel Leader

Sex:Male
He has been keeping track of four Atlas employees - Oz, Lilith Swann, Jim Decker and Kahn - for an unknown amount of time, possibly before their attempt to survive the outbreak at the Atlas research facility. When the four of them were brought back to an Atlas facility near a Burger Town restaurant, the Leader continued to monitor them, up to the point where they recovered and fought the undead forces in Burger Town. The Leader and his Sentinel troops then went in and saved them from a zombie horde, but held them at gunpoint. Upon meeting the survivors, he claimed that one of them "has to go".

Bubby
