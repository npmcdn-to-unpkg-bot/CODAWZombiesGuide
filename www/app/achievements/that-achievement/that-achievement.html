Mad Hops

20G/Bronze

Jump over 4 live sharks in 10 seconds in Carrier.
In order to do this step, it is required to kill zombies within the Moon Pool area, preferably within the three squares on the floor. The zombies' blood will leak through the floor, which will attract a shark. Once done, the player needs to jump over the middle circle of the room, where the shark will be swimming around, then make their way to the shark tanks in the Lift room and jump over them. Perform the task in under 10 seconds, and the achievement will then be unlocked.
