I'm Alive

10G/Bronze

Survive until round 10
This achievement is really easy. Just get to round 10 and that's all you have to do. The more players you have the more easily it is to get.

PC Load Letter

30G/Bronze

Use the 3D Printer 15 times in a single match

This achievement is easy. All you have to do is get 15000 credits (This could also get you the Moneybags achievement) and buy the box all at once. You could also just play the game and buy the 3D printer whenever you can. Most likely you will get the achievement if you're just trying to play because there is going to be some times where you get sucking guns or the gun you don't want.

Call in a rescue

Cheapskate

15G/Bronze

Acquire the exo suit without spending any money
*Note* this requires at least 2 players in order to get this achievement. This achievement is really easy. The people who are trying to get it can't buy anything what so ever. You at least need one person to buy all the doors towards the exo room. Whatever way you go is up to you. There are no cheap ways. You can go to the right side or the left side. You could also just play online and wait for people to buy the doors.

Moneybags

25G/Bronze

Have 15000 credits in the bank
*Note* The bank is how much you are holder on you. It's that simple. The best way to get this faster is to get a wall gun because some don't do that much damage as 3D printer guns. Also try to get ATMs or power boxes or credit orbital drops.

Survivor

25G/Bronze

Survive until round 25
This is like the "I'm Alive" achievement, but you have to get to round 25. Strongly recommend 2 or more players for people who are not that good at zombies because it is defficult to get to round 25.

Burgle Burgle Burgle

15G/Bronze

Steal 10 kills from a teammate
This achievement is somewhat easy. There are some times where you think you killed one of your teammate's zombie but you really didn't. To do this quick, grab a partner and let them get a distraction drone and then wants they throw it kill all zombies that are distracted by it and there you go you have the achievement.

Do You Even Exo?

15G/Bronze

Exo Melee kill 10 zombies in 30 seconds
This is easy. Just wait till you get a Hyper-damage power up. Then start knifing away until you get it.

Come On And...

25G/Bronze

Slam 10 zombies at once
*Note* You will need Exo-Slam, obviously, to get this. Fastest way to get this is to train at least 10 zombies or more, the more the better chance you have at getting it, and exo slam them.

20/20

40G/Bronze

Upgrade 2 weapons to level 20
This achievement is hard. Especially if you're new at zombies. The quickest way to get this is to buy one wall gun (keep your Atlas 25) and just try racking up some credits. Wants you have 2500 credits, you can get an upgrade on your gun at the upgrade station. *Note* You will need 95000 credits total.

Class Warfare

10G/Bronze

Play 30 minutes with each class online in Exo Survival
*Note*You can also get this on local play as well. You have to play with EVERY class for 30 minutes. The classes are The Light class, The Specialist class, The Heavy class and The Demolitions class. If you add up they all equal 2 hours. Unless if you want to wait for 2 hours doing nothing when there is a drone round. I suggest you play a different survival map because they last longer. You could even put a timer for yourself when to switch.

Exo Survivor

10G/Bronze

Successfully complete the Exo Survival Bonus Wave
On round 11 zombies will start showing up. Kill all of them till you can escape the riot map. Wants you do a cut scene will appear.
