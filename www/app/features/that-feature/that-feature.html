Power Switch

Credits Earned:100

There are multiple power switches spread throughout the map, and each one, when activated, will give the player access to certain features requiring power to be accessed. A power switch will give 100 credits to the player who activated it.

Features Include:
-All Perks -Exo suits
-Traps

Upgrade Station

Cost: 2500/Upgrade
Upgrades are permanent, meaning one can get rid of an upgraded gun and then buy it again off the wall or from the 3D Printer but without needing to start over with all the upgrades. Players can upgrade their weapons a maximum of 19 times per weapon up to Mk20. A secret experimental Upgrade Station can be unlocked by completing a certain part in the easter which will allow players to upgrade one more time to Mk25. Each time the player upgrades the gun, it will replenish ammo, gain a new skin, and get increased stats. In specific Mk levels, a new attachment may be added or maximum ammunition may be increased.
There are three Upgrade Stations on Outbreak. The first two are located in the Morgue and in the Administration, and the last one is found in the Exo Testing room after completing "Game Over, Man".

Orbital Care Packages

Orbital Care Packages will randomly appear during rounds.
Most of them will land in the starting area, although some may appear in the Exo suits Room after it has been opened.
A player can stack a maximum of three Care Package items. It will contain the following items:

-A Remote Sentry with the Rip able module, and possibly the Rocket Turret module or the Directed Energy module.
-A Aerial Assault Drone with the AI Control module and possibly the Rockets module.
-The Camouflage bonus.
-Credits. The amount of credits the player will gain will be randomized each time, ranging from 100 to 1000.
-Sentinel Reinforcements can defuse the bomb, revive players or kill zombies and atlas soldiers.


*Note* When you pick a credit package while multiplier is on. You will get twice the number you would have had.

Decontamination Zone

Cost:250 credits
During a Host wave; if the player is hit by a Host, they become "infected", and have a minute to be decontaminated, and every time the player is hit by a zombie while infected, the player will lose extra time from the counter. It will cleanse the player. It can also cleanse teammates while it is active. Upon being decontaminated, the player will gain invisibility from zombies for a few seconds. The device has a 10 second cool down time.
Located in the Administration room
Located in The Pit
Located in the Med bay and Moon Pool.

3D Printer

Cost: 1000 credits
The 3D Printer gives you a random weapon.

The 3D Printer visibly cycles through different weapons when idling, and upon purchase, the weapon currently on display becomes printed. This means the player can actually influence what weapon they receive. A bad thing is, the weapons switch very quickly making it difficult to receive ones desired weapon. The 3D Printer, if used enough times, will eventually malfunction, and another 3D Printer in another location will be activated.
Locations Are:
-Main Hub: In the central booth
-Holding: On a room to the left when entering the Holding from the Main Hub
-Hallway: In the hallway, close to the door to the Main Hub (default location)
-Morgue: Below the staircase leading towards the Exo Testing room
-Exo Testing room: Next to the staircase from the Morgue
Locations are:
The Pit: Next to the Decontamination Zone.
Atlas Command: In the far left building in the center (default location).
Hoard More: In a small room in the top left hand corner of the building.
Burger Town: In the main lobby of the restaurant.
Value Voltage: Inside the store, across from an Upgrade Station.

ATM

Credits earn:100
ATM's will give the player 100 credits each time they are used. Dispensers will be randomly activated during the game; any active one can be identified by its green screen. They will sometimes malfunction and start shooting out money. Range of 500 to about 1000 credits.

*Note* When multiplier is on, the ATM will give you 200 credits.

Grenade Disposal Machine

When you throw a grenade into the Grenade Disposal, the grenade will bounce off pegs like a Pachinko game, and land on a slot that will reward the player with either 100, 200, 300, 400, 500, 750, or 1000 credits.

Grenade Disposal is loacted in the Armory
