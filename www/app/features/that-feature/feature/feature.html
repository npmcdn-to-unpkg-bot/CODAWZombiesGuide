Insta Gator

Where:The Pit

Cost:500
It summons a alligator that will swim around the pit and devour any zombie that wonders around the water, instantly killing them.

It only kills one zombie at a time.

Make sure the power switch is on.

Orbital Strike

Where:Burger Town

Cost:500
The Orbital Strike launches missiles to the round killing the zombies in its radius. Red markers appear where a missile is about to hit.

It has a 60 second cooldown before it can be used again. Make sure the power switch is on.

Electrified Floor

Where:Starting area

Cost:500
The floor of the Hoard More will become electrified dealing damage to zombies in the area but not the player. It will produce an EMP to players that are in the room when activated.

Make sure the power switch is on.

Automated Snipers

Where:Atlas Command

Cost:500
Two automated snipers located on the towers in the area will activate, and will shoot at the zombies in the area.

Make sure the power switch is on.

Camouflage Spray

Where:Sewers

Cost:500
It will emit smoke giving you camouflage for a short period of time

Make sure the power switch is on.

Ambulance

Where:Value Voltage

Cost:500
It will trigger the ambulance's siren, that will attract zombies in the area and it will last for 15 seconds.

Make sure the power switch is on.
